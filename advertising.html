The Case for Ads

SEO Takes Months. Ads Start Working Tomorrow.

If you need leads now — not in 6 months — ads are the fastest path to a ringing phone.

With Google Ads, you show up at the very top of the results page the moment someone searches for what you offer. Not in the middle. Not on page 2. At the top, ahead of every organic result — including your competitors who've been doing SEO for years.

With Facebook and Instagram, you can put your business in front of specific people — by age, location, income, interests, and behavior. A roofing company can target homeowners in a specific zip code who are over 35. A salon can target women nearby who follow certain lifestyle accounts.

The downside of ads: you pay per click. The secret to good ad management is making sure every click is the right person, at the right time, seeing the right message — so your budget doesn't get wasted.

SEO vs Ads — which do you need?
Ads → I need leads THIS month

New business, no rankings yet, seasonal push, competitor just opened nearby.

SEO → I want free leads long-term

Established business, can wait 3–6 months, want sustainable free traffic forever.

Both → Maximum market coverage

Use ads for immediate revenue while SEO builds in the background. Best ROI overall.

FAQ

Advertising Questions, Answered Straight

How much should I spend on ads each month?⌄
For most local businesses, $300–$600/month in ad spend is a realistic starting point. That's enough to get consistent data and generate real leads. We can work with less, but very small budgets limit what's possible. We'll give you a specific recommendation for your market before you commit to anything.
How fast will I see results?⌄
Google Search Ads can start generating calls within the first 24–72 hours of going live. Facebook ads typically take 5–7 days to exit the learning phase and start performing efficiently. Most clients see their first leads within the first week.
Do I need a website to run ads?⌄
For Google Search Ads — yes, you need somewhere to send the traffic. A landing page or full site works. For Google Local Services Ads, you don't need a website. For Facebook Lead Ads, you can collect leads directly inside Facebook without sending anyone to a website. We'll tell you what you need for your specific situation.
Can I pause or stop the ads whenever I want?⌄
Yes. You own the ad accounts. Ads can be paused or stopped anytime. If you want to pause for a slow month or stop entirely, just let us know. There's no penalty and no contract. The ad accounts remain yours even if you stop working with us.
What makes a good ad?⌄
The right message, to the right person, at the right moment. Most businesses waste money with generic ads targeting everyone. We write specific ads aimed at your actual customer, with a clear offer and a single call to action. Then we test variations and keep what works.
