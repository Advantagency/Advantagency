FAQ

Hosting Questions, Answered Simply

What is web hosting exactly?⌄
Think of it like renting space on a powerful computer (server) that's connected to the internet 24/7. Your website files live there. When someone types your web address, they're connecting to that server and loading your site. Without hosting, your website doesn't exist online.
Can you move my existing site from another host?⌄
Yes, and we do it for free. We copy every file, every database, every email account over to our servers. During the migration your old site stays live so there's no downtime for your visitors. The switch usually happens overnight when traffic is lowest.
What happens if my site gets hacked?⌄
We've got you covered two ways. First, our security scanning catches most threats before they cause damage. Second, if something does get through, we restore your site from yesterday's backup — completely clean — usually within a couple hours.
Is there a contract?⌄
No. Month-to-month billing. Cancel anytime. If you cancel, we help you move your files to a new host so you're never stuck or left without your own site.
Do I need to do anything technical?⌄
Nothing. You never need to log into a server, touch a file manager, or deal with DNS records. We handle 100% of the technical side. The only time you hear from us is a monthly status email and if we proactively catch something that needs your attention.
