The Basics

What Is SEO and Why Does It Matter?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. In plain English: it's the work that makes Google show your business when someone searches for what you offer.

Think about what you do when you need a plumber, a restaurant, or a dentist. You pull out your phone and search Google. The businesses at the top get the calls. The ones on page 2 may as well not exist.

SEO is the ongoing process of telling Google: "This business is real, it's relevant, and it deserves to be at the top." It takes a few months to build momentum — but unlike ads, once you're ranking, the traffic is free.

There are two places you want to show up: the Map Pack (the 3 map listings that show first) and the organic results below it. We work on both.

Local search result anatomy
📍 Map Pack — we get you here first
3 map results that show above everything else. Driven by Google Business Profile.
🔗 Organic Results — long-term play
Regular website links below the map. Take longer, but deliver consistent free traffic.

FAQ

SEO Questions, Answered Plainly

How long until I see results?⌄
For Google Business Profile and map pack — often 4–8 weeks. For organic website rankings — typically 3–6 months. SEO is a long-term investment, not an overnight fix. The good news is that once you're ranking, the traffic keeps coming for free.
Can you guarantee a #1 ranking?⌄
No. Anyone who guarantees a #1 ranking is lying to you — Google controls rankings and nobody can guarantee them. What we can guarantee is that we do the right work, we're transparent about progress, and we deliver measurable improvement over time.
What's the difference between SEO and Google Ads?⌄
Ads put you at the top instantly but cost money every single click. When you stop paying, you disappear. SEO takes longer to kick in but builds lasting organic rankings that bring free traffic indefinitely. Most businesses benefit from running both — ads for fast leads, SEO for long-term free traffic.
Do I need a website for SEO?⌄
For Google Business Profile and map pack ranking — no, you can rank without a website. But having a website significantly boosts your ability to rank for organic search results and gives potential customers a place to learn more before calling. We recommend both.
What's a Google Business Profile?⌄
It's the free Google listing that shows your business on Maps and in local search results — the box with your name, phone number, hours, photos, and reviews. It's the single most important thing for local businesses to have set up and optimized. We handle the whole thing.
