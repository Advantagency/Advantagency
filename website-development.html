The Process

From Zero to Live in 7–14 Days

Here's exactly what happens after you reach out — no surprises, no delays.

1
Discovery Call (Day 1)

We hop on a quick call — or just chat over text/email if that's easier. We ask about your business, your customers, what pages you need, and what you want the site to do. No tech knowledge required on your end.

2
Design Mockup (Days 2–4)

We build out the look and feel of your homepage and send it over for feedback. You see exactly what you're getting before we build anything — colors, layout, copy, everything.

3
Full Build (Days 4–9)

Once you approve the design, we build every page, hook up your contact forms, set up SSL, optimize for speed, and write clean SEO-ready code. You don't lift a finger.

4
Review & Revisions (Day 10–11)

You get a preview link to click through the whole site before it's live. Tell us anything you want changed — text, colors, layout — and we fix it.

5
Launch 🚀 (Day 12–14)

We point your domain to the new site, submit it to Google Search Console, and you're live. We stick around for any questions and optional maintenance going forward.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to provide content (text, photos)?⌄
We can work with whatever you have. Got a logo? Great. Photos? Even better. No content at all? We'll write copy for your pages and use professional stock photos that match your business. Just tell us what you do and we handle the rest.
What platform do you build on?⌄
It depends on your needs. Most local business sites are built clean with HTML/CSS/JS for maximum speed — no bloated page builders. For clients who want to update content themselves, we use WordPress. We recommend what makes the most sense for your situation.
Do I own the website after it's built?⌄
Yes, 100%. You own all the code, the design, and the content. If you ever want to move to a different host or developer, everything is yours to take with you. No lock-in.
What if I need changes after launch?⌄
Minor changes (text updates, adding a photo) we can handle for free if they're quick. Larger changes or ongoing maintenance are billed at a flat hourly rate or rolled into a monthly care plan. We'll always be upfront about cost before doing any work.
Do I need hosting separately?⌄
We can bundle hosting for $29/month, or we can deploy your site to any host you already have. We also work with clients who want to host on Cloudflare Pages or similar — sometimes at zero monthly cost for simple sites.
